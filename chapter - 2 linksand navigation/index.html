About The Series

The Witcher is a dark fantasy drama based on the books by Andrzej Sapkowski. Set in a medieval-inspired land called The Continent, the show follows Geralt of Rivia — a mutated monster hunter — as he struggles to find his place in a world where humans can be more wicked than beasts.

Series Overview

The story explores intertwined destinies of Geralt, sorceress Yennefer of Vengerberg, and Crown Princess Ciri. Through bloody battles, twisted politics, and ancient magic, they battle fate itself. As war looms over The Continent, alliances shift and monsters rise — but destiny has a plan of its own.

Main Characters

  • Geralt of Rivia – The White Wolf, a witcher feared and respected
  • Yennefer of Vengerberg – A powerful sorceress with a tragic past
  • Ciri – Princess of Cintra, bound to Geralt by destiny
  • Jaskier – Bard and comic relief, Geralt's unlikely friend
  • Tissaia de Vries – Mage mentor and head of Aretuza
